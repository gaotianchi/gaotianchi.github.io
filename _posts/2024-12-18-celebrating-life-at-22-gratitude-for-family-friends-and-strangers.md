My internship is still ongoing, and today marks a special day in this journey—today is my 22nd year on Earth.

I want to take this opportunity to thank my family, who have been irreplaceable in my life. The virtues and imperfections I have inherited from them have made me unique and irreplaceable in this world.

When I was still in my mother’s womb, the country was strictly enforcing its [One-Child Policy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One-child_policy). As a second child, my mother was at risk of being forced into an abortion. To avoid this fate, my parents had to hide and seek refuge with relatives, which ensured that I was born safely.

According to my father, my birth was very difficult, to the point where the had to use a vacuum extractor to deliver me. Undoubtedly, they endured great fear and anxiety. I often imagine that scene: in some hospital, my mother holding me tightly, and my father guarding by the bedside, occasionally glancing at me, with a gentle smile on his face. This moment took place exactly 22 years ago today. At that time, my sister was about 8 years old.

My sister endured over a decade of illness. Though her condition was not fatal, she would never be free from the chronic pain for the rest of her life. However, the pain did not break her; instead, it made her stronger and more capable of embracing life with greater compassion. Two years ago, my lovely nephew was born, bringing new life and energy to our family.

I once complained about my father's apparent indifference toward my sister during her treatment, but I didn't expect my sister to be upset. She said to me, "Father may not express himself well, but he has always been silently supporting me, and I understand him." At that time, I was in a rebellious phase, and my dissatisfaction with my family consumed my mind. Her words opened a new door for me, allowing me to understand myself and my parents in a different way. Over the following years, my heart gradually found peace, and I believe it was her tolerance that helped me change.

In addition, I would like to thank all those who have helped me along my journey—my teachers, friends, and the many strangers who have shown me kindness. Among them, I want to especially thank my cousin, who cared for me attentively while I was searching for an internship. Many of my decisions were guided by the valuable experiences she shared with me. Thank you all!

Finally, happy birthday to me, Gao Tianchi!
