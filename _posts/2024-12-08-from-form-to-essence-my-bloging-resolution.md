The concept of blogging has been on my mind for years.

I have attempted numerous times to create a blog site, experimenting with various static site generators, and even managed to set one up successfully. However, despite having the site ready, I have consistently found myself stuck at the initial stage of generating content. I have grappled with selecting a theme and personalizing the style, yet I have never truly put pen to paper to produce a substantial piece of writing.

Looking back, I realize I made a common mistake: putting the cart before the horse. The essence of blogging is to record and express thoughts, not to showcase tools or pursue perfection in form.

Now, I have decided to make a change. I will no longer lose myself in the haze of tools and themes but will focus on creating solid content. High-quality content will be the essence of my blog, while form will merely serve to enhance the content. This post marks the beginning of a new chapter, documenting this fresh start.
