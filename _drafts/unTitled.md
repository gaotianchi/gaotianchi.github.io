博客这件事，这些年总是在我脑海中挥之不去。

我尝试过无数次开发博客网站，用过静态网站生成器，也成功搭建了一个。
然而，站点虽成，我却总卡在内容的起点。纠结于主题的选择、样式的个性化，
却始终没有真正动笔写过一篇像样的内容。

回首这段历程，我意识到自己犯了一个常见的错误——本末倒置。博客的本质是记录和表达，而不是炫耀工具或追求形式的完美。

如今，我决定做出改变。不再迷失在工具和主题的迷雾中，而是扎扎实实地创作内容。高质量的内容将是我博客的灵魂，而形式只需为内容服务。这篇文章，将作为第一篇内容，记录这一全新的起点。

The idea of blogging has lingered in my mind for years.

I’ve tried countless times to develop a blog site, experimented with static site generators, and even successfully set one up. Yet, despite having the site ready, I’ve always been stuck at the starting point of creating content. I struggled with the choice of theme and personalization of style, but never really put pen to paper to write a decent piece of content.

Looking back, I realize I made a common mistake—putting the cart before the horse. The essence of blogging is to record and express, not to show off tools or to chase perfection in form.

Now, I’ve decided to make a change. I’ll no longer lose myself in the haze of tools and themes but focus on solid content. High-quality content will be the soul of my blog, and form will merely serve the content. This post will mark the beginning of a new chapter, documenting this fresh start.
